💡 ICT Exam Study Tips

  • ✅ Start Early: Begin practice 3-4 months before your exam date
  • ✅ Understand Concepts: Focus on understanding rather than memorization
  • ✅ Practice Regularly: Solve 1-2 papers per week consistently
  • ✅ Review Mistakes: Learn from errors and understand the correct approach
  • ✅ Time Management: Practice with a timer to improve your speed
  • ✅ Join Study Groups: Collaborate with peers for better understanding
  • ✅ Take Breaks: Study for 45-50 minutes then take 10-minute breaks
  • ✅ Stay Healthy: Get adequate sleep and exercise for better focus
